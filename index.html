About the Hackathon

Hack Kigali is a youth-powered hackathon organized by Saint Vincent Pallotti Gikondo Hack Club. It’s an exciting opportunity for students and aspiring developers to build digital solutions focused on improving education and strengthening cybersecurity in Kigali and beyond.
